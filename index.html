Crystal Collector

By clicking on a crystal below you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the number in the box, you lose the game if your total score goes above the number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it and it add to your total. Each time the game starts you will try to reach a new number and the values of your crystals will change.

GOOD LUCK!
